Parkour

Parkour in Watch Dogs 2 allows players to navigate the vibrant city of San Francisco and its surrounding areas with agility and style. Marcus can climb buildings, leap over obstacles, and perform acrobatic maneuvers to quickly traverse the urban landscape.

Fire Combat

Watch Dogs 2 does feature firing combat as part of its gameplay mechanics. Players have access to a variety of weapons, including firearms, explosives, and non-lethal gadgets, which they can use to engage in combat encounters with enemies throughout the game.

Hacking

As Marcus explores the city, he can hack into various electronic devices such as drone and Remote Control car, manipulate traffic lights, control security systems, and access personal information to aid him in his missions.
